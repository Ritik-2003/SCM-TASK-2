DAV College

DAV College, Chandigarh, also known as DAVCHD, was founded in Chandigarh in 1958 by Mahatma Hans Raj. Affiliated to Panjab University, it is managed by Dayanand Anglo Vedic College Trust and Management Society. One of the prominent educational institutions under the DAV institution umbrella, the college derives its inspiration from Swami Dayanand Saraswati, founder of Arya Samaj and a leading visionary. DAV College Chandigarh has been accredited with 'A' Grade by the NAAC Bangalore, and is ranked as the top Commerce college in Northern India as per a survey by AC Nielsonorg-Marg. It also falls within the All-India rank Band 101-150 and has been titled as Chandigarh's Best College (2019-20). It has also been ranked 109 by NIRF in 2020.
DAV College, Chandigarh offers bachelor degree courses such as BA, BCom, BCA, BSc, BVOC along with PG courses such as MBE, MA, MSc. Moreover, the college also runs diploma courses in Cosmetology and Beauty Care, Medical Lab Technology, Computer Applications, Marketing Management and Mass Communication. It also offers PhD in Chemistry, Zoology and Biotechnology.

DAV College, Chandigarh has a robust infrastructure comprising library, labs, sports facilities, hostel accommodation for boys and girls, cafeteria, medical, banking, and other facilities. It also has an Instrumentation Lab for making institutional and industrial research feasible for all. It has also been selected under FIST Programme by Department of Science and Technology (DST), New Delhi and has been awarded a grant of Rs 1.5 crore for creating the required quality research infrastructure.

Chitkara University Admissions

DAV College Chandigarh offers admission to UG, PG, UG Diploma, and PG Diploma courses in various specialisations. DAV College offers courses under various streams such as Science, Arts, Commerce, and Computer Science, etc. The admission to most of the courses is based on the merit score in the last qualifying exam. For admission to the MSc, programme candidates must have cleared PU-CET (PG) conducted by the Panjab University. The admissions to DAV College, Chandigarh are given on the first-come-first-served basis seat availability. The application forms are issued online on the official website. The college offers various scholarships to the meritorious & reserved category students. As per the DAV College placements 2021, the highest package was recorded Rs 19.25 LPA. Some of the top recruiters were Focus, Edumatics, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, Infosys, and Wipro, etc.

Vision

  • To promote the foundational values like work culture, spirit of public service, inculcating discipline, dedication & determination in students.
  • To impart IT enabled education by providing a conducive atmosphere leading to intellectual, spiritual and academic growth of students with an aim to build professional attitude, communal harmony and nurture lofty ideals of Vedic Culture.
  • To empower the young students by providing them holistic education with emphasis on alignment of theory with practice, academia with industry and fostering the sense of enterprise with motivation and professionalism.

Mission

  • To give a practical shape to the ideals of Swami Dayanand's educational philosophy which aims to develop a modern scientific approach among the students, coupled with an understanding of their roots in the ancient Vedic Culture.
  • To provide a unique and different learning environment in the stream of Science, Commerce, Management, Humanities & Information Technology by imparting knowledge, skills, hands on practical training and industry exposure along with a strong value system in order to groom the students as competent, dynamic and socially awakened citizens of the country.
  • To impart to the students the best of new world humanistic, scientific and technical knowledge with the sense of history of their rich cultural heritage-the Anglo and Vedic values
