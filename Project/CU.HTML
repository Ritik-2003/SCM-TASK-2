Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University is a private University situated in the Union Territory Chandigarh. The University was established in the year 2012 and has gained a tremendous momentum in very few years. Chandigarh University is accredited by the Council of Architecture (COA), AICTE and UGC Approved University. The university is among Top 5% institutions of India for receiving NAAC A+ rating.
The university is popular for various discipline such as Engineering, Management, Architecture, Hotel Management etc. It has been ranked amongst Top 1.7% Universities of Asia and Overall 271-280 by QS University Ranking Asia.

Chandigarh University conduct CUCET Exam (Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test) for admission into various courses. Scholarship worth INR 33 crore are also given to students in form of tuition waivers based on CUCET Exam. The university has also introduced new namely R-Tikaram Para-Athlete Sports Scholarship for Para-Olympians worth INR 1 crore.

Chitkara University Admissions

Chandigarh University offers admission to undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses. The university has a common method of admitting the aspirants, through the Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test. Students can proceed with the Chandigarh University admission process after fulfilling the eligibility criteria. It is mandatory for students who wish to apply for Chandigarh University admission need to take the CUCET exam and achieve qualifying marks. The application process for Chandigarh University admissions is quite simple. Aspirants can either register online at the university website or go down to their office to register offline.
