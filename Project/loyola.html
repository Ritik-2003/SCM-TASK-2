Loyola College

Loyola College, Chennai is a Catholic Minority Institution offering admission in fields of Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Vocational Studies & so on located in a sprawling 99-acre lush green campus at Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Accredited with Grade “A++” by NAAC, and affiliated with the University of Madras, the college provides excellent & holistic education in commerce and business education at the undergraduate, postgraduate, and research levels.

Offering various UG, PG, and Research courses, the college imparts qualitative education with full-fledged faculties of Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Vocational Studies under its 8 departments. Admission to such courses is done either on the basis of merit scored in the last qualifying examination or through a university-based entrance examination.

Apart from providing excellent infrastructure, cultural and other extracurricular activities, the university aims to create successful professionals. The college provides top-notch, well-qualified, supportive, and dedicated faculty members to its students. The college has 210 administrative staff, 329 academic staff, 184 Ph.D. qualified faculty members.

Loyola College Admissions

The college offers online mode of application to the candidates through online admission portal. Candidates should fill-in all the details, upload required documents and submit the form. Email ID and Password is necessary for online registration.

How to Apply:

Step 1: Registration through Online Application Portal

Candidate should log on to the Online Application Portal and register by entering the following personal details such as applicant name, date of birth, mobile number, email ID etc. As soon as the registration is complete, students will be sent a “Username” and “Password” which will be their login credentials next time they log on to the registration portal.

Step 2: Filling-in the Basic and Specific details

Upon logging in through the provided credentials (Username and Password), candidates will be required to fill the application form with their basic details (Gender, Caste, Address) along with Specific Details (Special category, Differently abled) and educational qualifications (Subject, Marks etc)

Step 3: Attaching the Documents

Candidates should attach the scanned copies of the necessary supporting documents such as mark statement and community certificate. The scanned copies must be in black and white and must not exceed size of 200 kb.

Step 4: Payment of Application Fee

Candidates can pay the application fee through Credit Card/ Debit Card/ Net Banking or by Cash at any branch of Axis Bank (through Easy Pay) as per your convenience.

After the payment of application fee, an application number will be generated and the status will be sent to the candidate by SMS or through mail.

Loyola College Scholarships

Students of Loyola College can avail various scholarships from the government, various NGOs. Also, Loyola College aids students in getting part-time jobs (for the shift I students) and helps in getting various scholarships from government and non-government organisations. Loyola College keeps on giving notices for various scholarship opportunities through its website.

Few notable scholarships available for Loyola students are -

  • SC/ST/OBC Government Scholarship
  • Rama Kant Munjal Foundation Scholarship
  • Loyola Management fee concession scheme
  • Sheshan Scholarship
  • Nanmai Trust Scholarship
  • Nevett Scholarship
  • IVDP Scholarship

*These scholarships are indicative and Loyola keeps on adding new scholarship opportunities
