Lovely Professional University

With its ultra modern and huge campus on the National Highway that too at the entry of Jalandhar City, Lovely Professional University never fails to astound students seeking to become a part of it. The university has the single largest campus in the country with more than 30,000 students and 4000 faculty members and staff. It offers more than 200 programs at the undergraduate, postgraduate, and the doctorate level. The university has been recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has remained to be a member of Association of Indian Universities (AIU).

The curriculum of the university had been benchmarked internationally with more innovative pedagogy, experiential learning, and more with more than 200 international tie ups. These parameters have been established to achieve equitable and most importantly affordable quality education. The university has established itself as a Center of Excellence in Research.

The campus of the university exhibits a huge diversity as the students and other academic members belong to all the 29 states of the country and there are students from more than 70 countries in the world. The university manages to provide students with comprehensive learning and education which directly aims at developing the intellectual and personal strengths of students pursuing their career from the university. The students are taught and given experiences at both outside and inside of the classrooms; they are encouraged to nurture creativity and innovation inside them through challenging project works and participative learning. The university also ensures to provide its students with various favorable opportunities to sparking ideas and how they can translate their ideas into reality. Lovely Professional University has an excellent track record of instigate its students into their desired careers and this has already been proved by university's alumni who are working in more than 500 global and Indian super brands.

Admissions

The last date to apply for LPU NEST 2022 is January 25 (extended date) for BTech and for MBA (Schedule-II). For PhD, however, the last date for final admission 2022 was January 14.

LPU admissions 2022 is mostly based on LPUNEST 2022 exam for which are applications are currently open (till January 25). The University offers more than 300 degree and diploma programmes to aspirants at the UG, PG and doctoral levels along with several distance learning programmes via LPU Distance Education (LPUDE). LPU admission 2022 to each course follows specific admission criteria. LPUNEST is the official entrance and scholarship exam conducted by the University. In addition to this, Lovely Professional University accepts several other national-level entrance exams for admission to its UG and PG programmes. Last year, LPU started a special COVID-19 frontline scholarship which is an INR 20 crore support initiative. The University also made a record of 100% placements in 2020 and witnessed 20% rise in the number of companies participating the placements drive which concluded with the highest salary of INR 42 LPA.
