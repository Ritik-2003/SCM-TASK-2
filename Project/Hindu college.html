Hindu College

Acknowledged as one of the best Science, Arts and Commerce colleges in the country, Hindu College is a constituent college of Delhi University. Recently ranked 1 for Science and Arts and 2 for Commerce by India Today 2021, Hindu College completed its 120+ years of academic excellence in 2021. Accredited by NAAC, this college has set a 95% placements record for itself over the years. Earlier the college was affiliated with Panjab University and was moved under DU in 1922. For Hindu College admission 2021, the University will consider UG admission based on the merit of Class 12 this year, as declared by DU's VC. DU UG registrations 2021 began in the last week of July and first week of August for PG and UG courses, respectively.

Hindu College courses are available at both UG and PG levels in the field of Arts, Science and Commerce. Final selection to all the UG courses is based on DU cutoff lists released in seven to eight rounds, every year. On the other hand, PG admission to Hindu College is both merit and entrance-based. Candidates with a prior record of education from DU are eligible for merit-based admission to PG courses, while other university pass-outs are required to appear for DUET.
