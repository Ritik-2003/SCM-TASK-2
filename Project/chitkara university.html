Chitkara University

Chitkara University Admissions

  • Chitkara University Admissions are made through online process
  • The candidate can apply online for the respective courses at the portal.
  • For maximum courses, the admission will be made on the basis of 10+2 scores.
  • For B.Tech courses, JEE Main scores are needed.
  • For M.Tech courses, GATE scores are needed
  • For MBA courses CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT scores are needed.
  • For B.Arch course NATA scores or JEE Main Paper-2 scores are needed.
  • The admission to PhD courses is made on the basis of performance by the candidate in the Postgraduate course.
