Miranda House

Miranda House is a Delhi University constituent college situated in North Campus. Miranda House was established in 1948. It is an all women college offering courses in Science and Liberal Arts. In 2017 Miranda House was named as the Best College in India by NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework).

Miranda House is regarded as one of the oldest colleges of Delhi University with about 3000 students studying in the college and 564 staff (academic and administration). Miranda House provides all the amenities to the students like Hostel, Sports, good infrastructure and vibrant culture.

Miranda House Alumnae Association Scholarships

The scholarships are given annually to two Second Year students, one from the Science stream and the other from the Humanities/ Social Science stream. The Scholarships recognize both academic merit and economic needs. Selection is by a committee of faculty that includes members of MHAA. The aim of the Scholarships is to facilitate the education of bright students from less economically privileged backgrounds who deserve recognition for their academic performance.
